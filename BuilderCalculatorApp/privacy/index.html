Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

TechHive ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Building Calculator App (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Account Information

When you sign in using Google or Apple Sign-In, we receive:

  • Your name (first and last name)
  • Email address
  • Profile picture URL
  • Locale/language preference

We do not receive or store your Google or Apple password.

Calculation Data

When you use the App's calculator features and save reports, we store:

  • Report names and types (e.g., land area calculations)
  • Calculation inputs (dimensions, measurements)
  • Calculation results and outputs
  • Any notes you add to reports
  • Timestamps of when reports are created or updated

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate your identity and provide access to the App
  • Save and retrieve your calculation reports across sessions
  • Display your profile information within the App
  • Improve the App's functionality and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • JSON Web Token (JWT) based authentication
  • Secure token storage on your device

Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by the operating system.

4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party authentication providers:

  • Google Sign-In: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign-In: Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any other third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information and saved reports for as long as your account is active. You can delete individual reports at any time within the App. If you wish to delete your entire account and all associated data, please contact us using the information below.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data and account
  • Export your calculation reports
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App and updating the "Last updated" date above.
